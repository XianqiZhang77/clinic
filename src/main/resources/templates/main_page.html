Introduction for children from 0 ~ 6 months of age


If your child of 6 months to 5 years of age has been showing one or more symptoms on this list for less than 24 hours, it is recommended that you keep them at home and avoid contact with other people. After 24 hours, re-evaluate the situation to see if there is any change in their symptoms. If one or more symptoms persist for more than 24 hours, proceed with self-assessment.

- Children ages 0-5: Rectal temperature of 38.5 °C (101.3 °F) and above
- Children ages 6+: Oral temperature of 38.1 °C (100.6 °F) and above

- Sudden loss of smell without nasal congestion, with or without loss of taste     - Significant loss of appetite
- Great fatigue or Headache     - Generalized muscle pain (not related to physical exertion)

- Cough (new or worse)     - Shortness of breath, difficulty in breathing
- Sore throat     - Runny or stuffy nose

- Nausea     - Vomiting
- Diarrhea     - Stomach aches
